Privacy Policy

Daily Patriot  ·  Effective date: February 20, 2026

Daily Patriot is a mobile application for iOS and Android that displays daily inspirational quotes from America's founding era. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Daily Patriot does not collect, transmit, or share personal information.

The app stores two things locally on your device: your notification preferences and your subscription status. Neither is transmitted to any server operated by Daily Patriot.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Daily Patriot does not handle or store payment information. Purchases are subject to the privacy policies of the respective platform store.

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no user accounts.

Children's Privacy

Daily Patriot does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
